Headlines:

1. PQ went from worst (1.4 / 2.7 GiB/s) to ~15 GiB/s in both directions
   — the single biggest win from upstream magetypes-based SIMD dispatch.
2. HLG → Linear got +2.3× from the same refactor.
3. BT.709 and Gamma22 did NOT move — the upstream refactor didn't cover
   these TF kernels yet, or their polynomial shape didn't SIMDify as
   cleanly.

Note on Linear → HLG regression (29.44 → 19.9):

The baseline meta flagged this number as suspicious ("Linear→HLG
unexpectedly faster than sRGB") and hypothesized it was the scalar
quadratic early-exit for v ≤ 1/12 biasing results on our pseudo-random
test distribution. After the SIMD refactor the kernel is consistent
across input distributions; the observed 19.9 GiB/s is likely the more
honest number. Worth re-measuring with a real HDR luma distribution
before concluding it's a real regression.
